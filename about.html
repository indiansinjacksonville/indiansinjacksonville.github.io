About Indians In Jacksonville

I believe there is a better way to connect the Indian community together under one platform. What started as a way for me to stay informed about the events and Indian movie showtimes evolved into a Facebook group. A platform to share these information with the growing members from the Indian community. The problem was that so many Indian organizations were disconnected. By creating this website and the Facebook group, Indians In Jacksonville became the one-stop-shop of sources of information. It is in my hope that you will benefit from using this platform.
